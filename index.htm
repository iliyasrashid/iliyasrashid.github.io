About

Dr. Iliyas Rashid received his M.Sc. in Bioinformatics from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi (2006), and his Ph.D. in Biotechnology with a specialization in Computational Genomics from AMITY University (2018). His doctoral work was carried out at ICAR–National Bureau of Fish Genetic Resources (NBFGR), India.

He worked as a Research Associate at ICAR–NBFGR from 2010–2019 in the Centre for Agricultural Bioinformatics (CABin), contributing to national genomics projects. Since 2019, he has been with the Vector Genetics Laboratory, University of California, Davis/San Diego, focusing on the population genomics of malaria vectors and spontaneous mutation rate studies using high-depth sequencing.
